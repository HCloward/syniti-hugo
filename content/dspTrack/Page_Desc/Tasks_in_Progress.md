# Tasks in Progress

<div class="use">

Use this page while [Viewing Reports in
dspTrack™](../Use_Cases/View_Reports_in_dspTrack).

</div>

To access this page:

1.  Click <span style="font-weight: bold;">Track \>
    </span>**Reports** in the *Navigation* pane.
2.  Click **Tasks in Progress**.
