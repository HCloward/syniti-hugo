# My Positions

<div class="use">

Use this page to [view your dspConduct™
positions.](../Use_Cases/Manage_Positions.htm)

</div>

To access this page:

1.  Select **dspConduct \> Configuration \> My Settings** in the
    *Navigation* pane.
2.  Click the **Positions** icon.
